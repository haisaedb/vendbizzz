About BNB Vending Solutions

Welcome to BNB Vending Solutions, a family-owned business dedicated to providing exceptional vending machine services and making a positive impact in our community. Founded by Bowie and Blackman, BNB Vending Solutions is fueled by a passion for entrepreneurship and a desire to give back.

Our Story

BNB Vending Solutions began as a shared dream between Bowie and Blackman to create a business that would not only provide for their family but also give back to the community. With a deep-rooted commitment to social responsibility, they set out to build a company that would make a positive impact beyond profits.

Our Commitment

We are committed to giving back to our community in meaningful ways. Whether through charitable donations, volunteer efforts, or partnerships with local organizations, we are dedicated to making a difference and enriching the lives of those around us.

Service

How we operate

1. Free Consultation

Contact us and we will schedule a meeting that is convenient for you so that we can visit your establishment and see where we can assist you with your vending needs.

2. Proposal and Agreement

After the consultation, we'll prepare a detailed proposal outlining our tailored solution for your business, including machine placement, product selections, etc. Once you are satisfied with the proposal, we will finalize our agreement.

3. Machine Setup

Our team will handle all aspects of vending machine setup. We'll coordinate the delivery and installation of the vending machine(s) at your location, ensuring that they are strategically placed for maximum visibility and convenience. Our goal is to make the setup process as smooth and hassle-free as possible for you.

4. Product selection and Stocking

While we discuss our product selections in detail during our proposal/agreement phase we value input from our location owners and their staff. We'll ensure your vending machines are routinely stocked with fresh and undamaged items.

5. On going Support and Maintenance

Our commitment to you doesn't stop after the setup phase. We provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure that the vending machine(s) operate smoothly and efficiently.
